Current Issues in India - 2025

A glance at challenges and opportunities shaping the future

AI & Automation Impact

India is rapidly adopting AI and automation, transforming industries. While this boosts efficiency, it also calls for major upskilling initiatives.

Mental Health Awareness

Mental health, especially among youth and professionals, is gaining attention. Increased awareness, counseling, and support systems are vital.

Urban Flooding

With rising urbanization and poor drainage planning, cities like Delhi and Mumbai face severe waterlogging. Infrastructure upgrades are essential.

Digital Privacy

As more Indians go online, concerns about data privacy and surveillance are growing. A robust data protection law is the need of the hour.
